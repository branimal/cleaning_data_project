# Data Dictionary
## subject
Id identifying which of 30 subjects record relates to. 
Values range from 1 to 30
## activity
Enumeration identifying which of six activities subject was engaging in when record was captured
Values are limited to:
* WALKING
* WALKING_UPSTAIRS
* WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
* SITTING
* STANDING
* LAYING

## values
The remaining 66 columns are the means of the values captured by the devices for the given subject performing the given activity during the experiment. The column names is composed as follows:

>signal-computation-direction

###Signal is one of the following values:
* tBodyAcc
* tGravityAcc
* tBodyAccJerk
* tBodyGyro
* tBodyGyroJerk
* tBodyAccMag
* tGravityAccMag
* tBodyAccJerkMag
* tBodyGyroMag
* tBodyGyroJerkMag
* fBodyAcc
* fBodyAccJerk
* fBodyGyro
* fBodyAccMag
* fBodyAccJerkMag
* fBodyGyroMag
* fBodyGyroJerkMag

###Computation is one of the following:
* mean()
* std() 

###Direction, where relevant, is one of the following:
* X
* Y
* Z
